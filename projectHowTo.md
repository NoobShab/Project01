## [3] Hashtag Research
You get to research and study specific hashtags based on their usage in content.

#### What's the purpose of this choice?
- This choice allows you to analyze reels based on your searched hashtag. 
- You get the data based on the number of reels analyzed with that hashtag
- It showcases the number of total views and engagements generated with the hashtag.
- It evaluates the average engagement generated across the social platform on the hashtag.

### How to:
1. Get in the CLI.
2. In the beginning it would ask for:
```
Enter hashtag: 
```
3. Enter your desired hashtag.<br><br>
3.1 Get data based on your input. For example; 
```
// Input:
Enter hashtag: fitness

// Output:
Reels analyzed: 81
Total views: 84,359,158
Total engagement: 11,304,621

Average engagement: 139,563
```
3.2 If any of your input is not contained in the '.csv' file, it'll output:
```

```
